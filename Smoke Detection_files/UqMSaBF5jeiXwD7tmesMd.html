what is smoke detection

Smoke detection is the process of identifying the presence of smoke particles in the air. It is a crucial component of fire safety systems as it helps in early detection of fires. Smoke detectors are devices that are designed to sense smoke particles and trigger an alarm to alert occupants of a potential fire hazard.
